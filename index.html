CrystalCollector!

1st You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

2nd There are four crystal below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of point to your total score.

3rd You win the game by matching your total score to a random number , you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

4th The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.
